Integrated
Design-Build Solutions.

NAX delivers fully integrated design-build electrical solutions across all disciplines — power distribution, fire alarm, CCTV and surveillance, access control, and structured cabling. Built from
the ground up.

NAX was founded in 2022 by Juan Pimentel — an electrician who started studying the trade in high school and spent nearly two decades working every role from journeyman to Chief Estimator. The estimating discipline, the communication standards, the closeout process were not adopted from a template. They were built from years of doing it on both sides of the contract.
