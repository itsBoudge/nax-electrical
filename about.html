NAX was founded in 2022 by Juan Pimentel, an electrician who started studying the trade in high school and spent nearly two decades working every role from journeyman to Chief Estimator before going out on his own.

The estimating discipline, the communication standards, the closeout process were not adopted from a template. They were built from years of doing it on both sides of the contract.

That foundation is why we move with the urgency of a growing firm and the discipline of a seasoned one. We communicate proactively, flag issues before they cost you, and show up ready to solve problems — not report them.

That same commitment to family shapes how we treat every client and every project. When you work with NAX, you work with a team that takes pride in the work the same way you take pride in your building.

How We Operate

The way we
run every project.

01
Proactive Communication

We flag issues before they cost you. Your project team is never caught off guard — scope variances are identified early, communicated clearly, and resolved without disruption to schedule.

02
Disciplined Estimating

Accurate, fully loaded quotes returned promptly. No low-ball numbers that balloon at closeout. We build complete scopes from your plans and own the work from rough-in through certificate of occupancy.

03
Clean Closeout

At closeout you receive complete documentation — as-builts, test reports, warranties, and a punch list that is actually completed. No chasing us down for paperwork after the project ends.

04
Single-Source Accountability

Fewer trade gaps, faster decision-making, reduced risk. One contract covering electrical, fire alarm, access control, structured cabling, and CCTV — with one team accountable for all of it.

That dual perspective — knowing exactly what it costs to build something and exactly what it takes to install it — is what makes NAX different. We speak the language of the GC and the language of the inspector, because we've been on both sides of the table.

When you reach out, you speak directly with an estimator or project manager who can address your scope immediately. No gatekeeping, no handoffs to someone who wasn't in the room.
